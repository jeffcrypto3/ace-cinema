About Ace Cinema

Where movie magic comes to life

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Ace Cinema was born from a passion for bringing the ultimate movie experience to our community. What started as a single-screen cinema has grown into a premier entertainment destination, featuring state-of-the-art technology and world-class hospitality.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable cinematic experiences by combining cutting-edge technology, exceptional comfort, and outstanding service. We believe every visit should feel special, whether you're catching the latest blockbuster or enjoying a classic film with family.

What Sets Us Apart

From our luxurious Cube experience with reclining seats and in-seat dining, to our immersive Dolby Atmos sound system, we've invested in creating the perfect environment for movie lovers.
